Sarah Hussain Speaker

APJ Abdul Kalam quoted “Dream is not that which you see while sleeping it is something that does not let you sleep” and our next speaker thrives on the same credence. She stepped on an unconventional profession path and haven’t receded ever since. The journey from a middle class teenage girl to One of India’s Top Food Bloggers hasn’t exactly been a bed of roses. The Voyage was pervaded with thorns but she bloomed like a bright and beautiful flower that she is. Self-Doubt, Society or even the Pandemic couldn’t cessate her passion for her work. We are more than ecstatic to present the woman whose kitchen is no less than Aladin’s Chirag.

Geet Speaker

Any amount of appreciation or exaltation falls short to portray the determination and grit of our First speaker. One can only imagine what a 10 year old girl endures when she wakes up to the fact that she cannot ever walk again. A Spinal Cord injury changed her Destiny forever but that did not deter her from dreaming big. She dreamt big and achieved bigger. From being India’s first Wheelchair-bound Actress to running multiple social media platforms, our speaker is a jack of all trades. She left her job in the US to come to India and work with children and at-risk Youth. She currently influences more than 7 lakh lives through her Instagram. Let us hear the voyage of the woman whose feet couldn’t defy her from taking steps towards her passion
