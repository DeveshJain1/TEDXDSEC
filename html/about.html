ABOUT US

We at TEDxDSEC, passionately believe in the power of ideas that can change attitudes, lives and ultimately the world. Breaking traditions and unleashing ideas we present to you the first ever edition of TEDxDSEC. It’s a grassroot initiative to magnify the wealth of knowledge and pave a way to wisdom to the youth by the young minds of Dyal Singh Evening College (A full fledged day college).

TED

TED is an American conference that talks on various topics under the slogan ideas worth spreading in the form of short, powerful interactive speeches (within 18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference which covered issues related to Technology, Entertainment and Design. TED is owned by non profit, non partisan foundation, their agenda is to make great ideas accessible and spark conversation.

TEDx

TEDx is a grassroots initiative, created in the spirit of TED’s overall mission to research and discover ideas worth spreading. TEDx brings the spirit of TED to local communities around the globe through TEDx events. Such events are organized by passionate groups of individuals or institutions that seek to uncover latest ideas and to share the researches made in local areas as well as broader topics that spark conversations in the community.
