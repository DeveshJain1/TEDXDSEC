More About TEDxDSEC

MISSION

TEDxDSEC aims at creating a community, welcoming people from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the world. We believe passionately in the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives and, ultimately, the world. Through our TEDx Talk event we want to build a clearinghouse of free knowledge from our countries' most inspired thinkers — and a community of curious souls to engage with ideas and each other, as per the feasibility of the global situation we are taking over our event to an online platform for ideas to flourish and change lives.

PURPOSE

Our purpose is same as the motto of TED Talks: “Ideas worth spreading”. We wish to spread ideas that inspire and motivate our audience. We want to bring together people from all corners of the world - people from all walks of life and from different cultures and places. Our talks will be aimed to provide platform for speaker's vision and idea to spread amongst the youth. Through our speakers who are inspirational personalities in their field we wish to help our audience to experience new thoughts, ideas and perspectives. So, the ideas and knowledge one gains from these TEDx Talks can be used to lead a better life.
